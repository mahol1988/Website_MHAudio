— About

Sound that moves people.

Matt Hollis is a freelance audio engineer and mixer based in the UK, working across advertising, broadcast, documentary and film. With a keen ear for detail and a collaborative approach, every project receives the full sonic attention it deserves.

From intimate documentary mixes to high-profile commercial campaigns, the work spans a broad range of formats — always with the same commitment to craft and storytelling through sound.

Clients range from leading advertising agencies and production companies to independent filmmakers and broadcasters. Whether working in the studio or remotely, the goal is always the same: sound that elevates the work.
